Need Some Great Entertainment?

Magician, Jack Palur will be happy to entertain you and your guests with professional prestidigitation at your next party or event.

Jack uses irreverent comedy and story telling to present close-up magic in a very entertaining way.

Jack has been entertaining audiences with sleight of hand for over 45 years. He performs for kids as the character "Professor Fineous." Jack also plays Amil Kaputska in the stage show,"The Kaputska Brother's Extraordinary Magical Extravaganza!"

Jack has taught magic for the past 15 years, founded several magic clubs, and has been performing in local clubs and pubs for most of his life. He is a member of both the International Brotherhood of Magicians and the Society of American Magicians. He and his family live in Sheffield Lake, Ohio.

With an extensive repertoire of close-up sleight of hand, Jack can bring intimate and audience involved entertainment to your next private party, corporate event, or restaurant/pub. He requires no stage, electricity, or set up of any kind. He hops table to table, room to room, guest to guest, all night long - using items like playing cards, coins, and other common items right out of his pockets!

It is simply astounding to see magic right under your nose! Give him a call right away! Dates are being booked fast!
