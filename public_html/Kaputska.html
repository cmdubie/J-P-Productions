The Kaputska Brothers

"The Kaputska Brothers Extraordinary Magical Extravaganza" is a traveling magic stage show featuring the talents of Magician Amil Kaputska and his sidekick brother, Bubutski Kaputska. The two intrepid performers have pieced together and entertaining show of magic and mayhem that includes music, comedy, stage illusions, mind reading, and lots of audience participation.

Although they may not have a complete grasp on the English language, the Kaputska brothers do eventually do real magic on the stage, performing feats of legerdemain and skilled prestidigitation. Well, sort of. . . Now if they could just get a handle on their little drinking problem. . . This is a high energy show that borrows heavily from vaudeville, has a dash of cabaret, and reeks of comedy mayhem. What these guys will do for a green-card is astonishing! This is simply a hysterically funny show!

You see with eyes and believe, yes?!?!

Many good things we do for you!

The Kaputska Brothers Extraordinary Magical Extravaganza is a full evening stage show that lasts around one hour. This is perfect entertainment for corporate banquets or other company events. Audiences can range in size from as few as 20 to up to 500 people or more. The Kaputska Brothers can work colleges, thater venues, comedy clubs, military bases, community theaters, and any other location that has a stage or large auditorium. Keep in mind the show is rated PG-13 for general bawdiness (nothing really offensive). There is a lot of audience participation.

The Kaputska Brothers have their own sound system that can integrate into existing sound equipment or they can use what the venue offers. Although a stage is not required, they do need a space that is at least 15 feet by 15 feet to perform on with audiences seated "theater style." Stage lighting and curtains are preferred but not required. The audience will need access to the stage for volunteer participation. We frequently work with warm-up acts like local comedians.

The Kaputska Brothers Extraordinary Magical Extravaganza combines slapstick, comedy magic, and hilarious shtick from two very creative entertainers. The combination of magic, music, and comedy is quite extraordinary. The duo has over 45 years of entertaining!
