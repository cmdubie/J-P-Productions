Speaking Engagements

Jack Palur, a Magician, Theater Director, and Entertainment Producer in Northern Ohio for the last 45 years, offers a 45 minute guest lecture that is funny, full of magic, and provokes thought. This lecture has audience participation, and centers on how magic enriches lives both at home and in the work place.

Follow Jack's talk as he explains how he started in the magic business, and where it has brought him today. The speech is perfect for any size group or organization, and is recommended for the ages teens to seniors.

Mr. Palur's lecture is appropriate for all organizations that are looking for something new and fun to entertain and educate their members. This talk is great for such organizations as Rotary Clubs, Amvets, Elks, Moose, 4H, Masons, Chamber of Commerce, Lions, Shriners, church groups, and all other community or fund raising clubs.

Don't forget, this lecture is also perfect for large or small corporate meetings.

About

Jack has been teaching and peforming magic for the last 40 years. He owns and operates JP Productions, a talent agency in Northern Ohio. As an entertainer he performs close-up magic for pubs, restaurants, and private parties. As Amil Kaputska, he is one half of the comedy magic team of "The Kaputska Brothers." He also plays "Professor Fineous Mildew-heimer," a children's comedy magician.

Jack has taught theater and directed over 25 theatrical productions. He has taught magic at summer camps and for youth organizations.

Previously, he owned and operated an improvisational company for 10 years, owned and operated a large retail hobby store for 17 years, and was a movie critic on a local television station.

Jack has a Degree in Liberal Arts and Business and a Technical Degree in Broadcasting.

Currently, Jack resides in Northern Ohio with his wife, daughter, and cat, Ming.
