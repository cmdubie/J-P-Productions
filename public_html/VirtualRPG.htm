Virtual Role Play Gaming

The coolest and most creative way to role play! Combining table top gaming with 3D live action, virtual gaming is a cinematic experience where the only limit is your imagination!

Game Masters and Players of any game system or experience level are welcome!

What We Offer:

- Cinematic encounter areas for all game systems, including medieval fantasy, sci-fi, western, horror, dark future, steam punk, apocalyptic, and many more!

- Free game tools, including cool game rooms, dice sets, name titlers, avatar outfits and weapons, and much more!

- Players and Game Masters from all over the world!- Convenient and flexible dates and times so you can run or play in a game at your convenience.

- In-world voice capabilities, or use your voice application of choice, like Skype or Discord.

- Any game system can be used! Links in-world to free, on-line game rules and character sheets!

- Pre-built game areas from all your favorite and classic role play genres, including Ravenloft, Desert of Desolation, Tomb of Horrors, and many more!

- We can build adventure areas for you to help you run your game campaigns!- We have a very friendly and experienced Staff that will help you with what ever issues might arise.

- Oh, and it is all FREE!

How To Get On The Grid:

- You need a viewer to log into the virtual grid. We recommend this viewer: Firestorm. Download and install it to your PC. It is free and safe. Be sure to DL the appropriate version for your system. The most common version is the "For SL & Opensim 64 bit"

- Create an account on the Virtual Highway grid. Here is the web page to do that: Virtual Highway. Follow the instructions there to create an avatar and select a name. An e-mail verification will finish this short process. If you have any issues during this process, contact the "live support," available on the web page.

- To allow your viewer to access the Virtual Highway grid, you need to tell the viewer to include Virtual Highway. Go here for instructions on how to to this: VH on Firestorm. (Scroll down to the Firestorm instructions.)

- We recommend the "Phoenix" skin, available in the lower right corner of the viewer's log in screen, under the drop down menu there.

- Log in to Virtual Highway! Using the viewer, enter your avatar's name and password and log into the grid. You will start at the welcome landing and are free to explore that area and get used to moving around. (See below.)

What To Do Once Your Are In-World:

- You can adjust your preferences in the Firestorm viewer any way you wish. (Under the drop down menu at the top of your viewer - "Avatar/Preferences.") Learn how to walk, run, fly and generally move around in the 3D environment. Learn how to use your camera controls to pan around and view things. (Avatar/Camera Controls.) Find your inventory and get a good idea on how things work there. (Bottom of Screen - Inventory.)

- Contact Zoltan Mint once in-world, (use the Search button near bottom of screen and search Zoltan Mint under the "People" tab, then "Send Message.") Let him know you have arrived. If he is on-line, he will help you navigate and get involved in the role playing areas of the grid. If he is not on-line, send him a message and he will receive it in his e-mail and get back to you shortly.

- We will help you find a more appropriate avatar, role play outfits, equipment, weapons, etc.. for what ever game you want to play. We will take you to the game hub, where you can find the free dice, titler, and other tools you might want, to role play games in Virtual Highway.

 

What You Need to Role Play Virtually:

- You need your own rule system. You can play any role play game you want here! We have areas designed for such classic role play games like Dungeons & Dragons, Shadowrun, Star Wars, Star Trek, Deadlands, Pathfinder, Call of Cthulhu, Traveller, Cyberpunk, Vampire, Champions, Rifts, Middle Earth, and many, many more! The game rooms have links to many rule systems that you can use, should you choose to run a game in Virtual Highway.

- You need to visit the Game Hub. There, you will find links to 100's of encounter areas, ready for your use to role play in! These areas are generic enough that you can use them for what ever setting you desire. You will find old west towns, fantasy dungeons, Victorian horror locations, sci-fi space stations, and many other locals, just waiting for you to explore! We have even recreated some of the classic D&D adventures in 3D for you convenience. Ask the staff to give you links to these ultra cool adventure locations!

- A newer computer with a good graphic card is highly recommended. Virtual gaming is a bit demanding on processors, so be sure to upgrade your equipment to experience the best possible experience.

- You will need a reliable Internet connection. We recommend using a directly connected ethernet cable, and not Wifi. Wifi is possible, but you may experience voice issues in-world. The faster your modem, the better.

- You will need a working microphone and speakers attached to your PC. These can be purchased cheaply either on the Internet or at your local electronic stores. Being able to speak to fellow players is necessary for a smooth role playing experience. We recommend a headset that has both ear speakers and microphone built in.

- You will need a group of players. We have many people willing to jump into a game, from all over the world, but we recommend that you find your own group of players, and invite them into Virtual Highway. Ideas on players include people you have not seen in awhile or that may have moved away. Virtual gaming is awesome in the fact that you can reconnect with old friends you may have not gamed with in a log while!

- So decide what game you want to run or play in, find a group of players, schedule a regular meet up, and play role playing games with the world, sitting in the comfort of your own home! For further information about the available genres and adventure settings in VH, click here.
