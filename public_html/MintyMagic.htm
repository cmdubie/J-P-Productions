Zoltan Mint has been performing virtual magic on Second Life for over 3 years! His show includes rezzed out and working stage illusions, and a dash of old fashion joke telling. This show is a throw-back tribute to the old burlesque, vaudeville like shows of the days gone by. His show is perfect for all venues and all audiences. Contact Zoltan today for booking info!


LOGISTICS

Zoltan's show is approximately 45 minutes long, and is a non - stop, fun filled extravaganza! The show has rezzed magical illusions, funny humor, and lots of audience participation.

Zoltan's act is typed-chat based, with full audience interaction. Because of the stream is needed, but if you would like to play music underneath the show, that is fine. We suggest non-lyric based, or instrumental background music, like jazz.

Zoltan works for a minimum of 100% of show tips. You heard right! Just tips. Of course, Zoltan appreciates tips from the venue owners if they are so inclined. Never necessary, but always appreciated! :)

The show also needs approximately 80 extra open, temporary prim slots for the illusions to be rezzed. Although a stage is optimal, he can work in any area that offers roughly a 20m x 20m space. He will bring his own tip jars and tip poster. A stage can be provided by Zoltan if there is additional prim allowance.

Please book early - give him at least a 1 week lead for any dates. Times and dates are preferred in PDT/SLT . Zoltan can work around venue's schedule, but because of a needed 30 minute of set up time, its best if he performs first so not to bother other performers setting up.

Let's Have Some Fun! :)


Zoltan's Bio

Zoltan has been performing magic in real life for the last 40 years. He lives near Cleveland, Ohio and offers a kid's show, a close up show, and a big stage comedy show for large venues. His specialty is close up magic, but that does not really work in SL. So he does large illusions in SL, accompanied by knee-slapping humor. He has been performing on SL since his first week on! He also teaches close up magic and is an avid movie fan in real life. He is older than dirt, and only slightly older than his teeth!
