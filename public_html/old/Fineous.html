Who the Heck is Professor Fineous?

Magician Jack Palur plays “Fineous Mildew-Heimer,” the irreverent and comical Professor of Archeology and World Explorer.

Equipped with adventuring tools, the Professor has traveled all over the world to find magical treasures that have the most amazing experimental results!

The Professor and his Assistant will have your kids involved in most of the tricks. He uses sidesplitting comedy appropriate for children of all ages. It is uproarious fun!

The Professor's show includes atmospheric music, realistic and magical puppets, and, of course, lots of cool magic illusions! This is great family fun!

Mr. Palur has been entertaining as a magician for over 40 years! Over 1000 show performed all over Ohio!

Please book early, for the Professor schedules weeks in advance!

About the Show!

The show runs about 45 minutes with no breaks. This performance is perfect for all ages, but children between 3 - 12 love it the most! It was designed for birthday parties, scouting events, class room rewards, summer programs, holiday events, library events, or any occasion where you want some extra special magic and fun!

The Professor brings along many of his animal friends that he has met along in his journeys. Near the end of the show, your kids will be really surprised when the Professor produces a 150 pound baby elephant puppet! This show stealer is named “Packy,” and after some comical antics, Packy actually performs some magic too!

The performance has comedy, music, puppetry, audience participation, and of course, lots and lots of magic! This is a high energy show! Kids really love the Professor and his friends! His assistant, Miss Penny Wiggle, will help you get the kids settled to enjoy the show. The audience will be "spellbound" for the entire length of the performance and remember it for a life time!

Let the Professor make YOU your child's hero by having him perform at their next party!
