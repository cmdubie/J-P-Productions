You’re Guide to Casa de Angeles

Welcome to Casa de Angeles! (Home of Angles) Here you will find everything you need to work, live and play in the old west! Casa de Angeles is a small desert town, set on a long lonely desert road somewhere in Arizona. Let the following be your guide, and hopefully you can explore the town and have a great visit!

THE GRANDE PALACE SALOON
This grand old building is both a bawdy saloon and a hotel. On the ground floor, you will find an active performance stage where many a saloon gal will dance the cancan. There are poker tables, a full service whiskey bar, and a player piano for whooping up a good time! Upstairs, there are 4 fully functioning bed rooms for guests, including a double bed with some naughty surprises!

THE BANK
This secured location holds all the gold found in the mines near by, as well as payroll for the cowboys and miners. On accession, the bank also holds important papers brought in from trains passing through. Want to rob the bank? Others have tried and failed - but will you succeed?

THE GENERAL STORE
This small shop has everything one would want to eat, wear, or shoot! The grumpy old proprietor sells dried goods, canned goods, guns, ammo, meat, vegetable, clothes, candy, and you can even get a saspirrila here!

MINTY GOODS WESTERN SHOP
This shop is an actual business, selling real Second Life products. You can purchase western clothes here, including hats, men's outfits, and women's outfits. Just click on the arrows on the vendors to scroll through the many available products!

SHERIFF'S OFFICE
Here the town Sheriff does his work. This building also houses the town's jail. Don't cross the Sheriff, or you may end up in this hooskow! You might want to check out the all the wanted posters in the main office, just to make sure your mug is not on any of them!

TELEGRAPH OFFICE
This small building is the town's communication center to the rest of the world. A secretary will send or receive a telegraph up to as far as New York City! (For a small fee.)

THE TRAIN STATION
This station sees locomotives pulling cars filled with either people or goods. The box cars are always loaded with supplies for Casa de Angeles, as well as other far off western towns. The passengers are either visitors in town for a short stop over, or permanent residents, looking for gold in them thar hills.

THE CAMP
Across from the Saloon, and behind Minty Goods, you will find a small campfire where you and your fellow cowpokes can gather around a fire and eat, rest, or chat about your wild times in the old west

.


About Role Playing in Casa de Angeles

Everyone is welcome to role play in Casa de Angeles. You will find all the amenities to make your role playing experience fun and exciting!

Rules and Regulations

- Please abide by both the Larimore Estate and Sheffield Parcel's Covenant. You will find the covenants in-world under "World/About Land/Covenant." Read them, understand them and there will be no problems!

- Casa de Angeles is open to all, for both "open play" role playing, as well as scheduled, Game Master ran events. If there is a conflict of availability of space or time, the scheduled games will have priority. Please respect these groups and leave them alone. There are many scheduled games available for you to play in here. Just ask the Game Masters when and where they get together!

- Casa de Angeles has been designed for any of the following role play scenarios:

- Old West RPGs
- Weird West RPGs (Deadlands)
- Cthulhu West RPGs
- West World RPGs (or other time travel adventures)
- Civil War Adventures
- Sidewinder Adventures
- Boot Hill RPGs
- Any other old west settings

- Please respect the eras above and dress, role play, and act accordingly. There are many fine shops around the grid that sell appropriate clothing and gear. Try to "play the part" and bring these exciting and adventurous times to life here on this sim! Casa de Angeles was deliberately created to be some what "generic," to allow any game to play here.

- Absolutely no pedophilia, griefing, spamming, too many attached scripts, open nudity, open sexual acts, excessive swearing, sexual solicitations, and anything else that would disrupt in other's fun.

- The Damage function may be turned on occasionally. You will know when it is on when you see a small heart on the top of your viewer's screen. Please IM Zoltan Mint if you desire this feature turned on.

- Please get permission from Zoltan Mint if you wish to implement the use of combat HUDs. Though we do not mind these HUDs, we would like to know when they are being used.

- Please refrain from flying, teleporting, and camming while here. (These items are not turned off, but by not doing them, you are preserving the atmosphere!)

- Please do not bother the shoppers or shop owners in any of the commercial shops on the ground, under the Space Station. . Further more, please stay out of the private homes. Those are private residencies that can be rented if desired.

- Please donate what you can. If you like this place and use it to play in, your donations will help keep it open!

- Have fun and play nice! This is what Second Life was meant for!
