Sheffield has many cool businesses that sell all types of desirable products! Everything from clothes to games, with shops specializing in era-specific products, as well as the latest gadgets on the grid! Make sure you visit all the shops for special freebies, promotions, and other great deals! Sheffield's shops are open 24/7, so stop by and time!


Maya Moonwing's Sweet Temptations Clothing Store

Maya's store offers the best of Second Life fashion from reputable companies. Sweet Temptations and SLC are two premier names in clothing - mesh (with demos), casual, evening wear and even something for your sexy side! Don't worry I haven't forgotten the fellas - with a whole floor of affordable fashions! Come see me today!


Candace Bethune's The Dubious Armoire Vintage Clothing Store

The Dubious Armoire offers day and evening wear for the discriminating lady. The shop offers ladies fashions of the 1920s and 30s, with a cheeky twist.

Look your best at the speakeasy!


Zoltan Mint 's Costume Creations Costume Store

This shop offers all types of cute, sexy, and realistic costume outfits for all your party and contest needs! Zoltan has 100's of costumes, ranging from Kermit the Frog to sexy French Maids! Each costume comes complete with the outfit, foot wear, special wigs and other add on prims if needed. And the prices are great! Contact Zoltan if you are looking for something not on the racks!


Florian Blaisdale's Floriana Curiosities

This shop has era-specific buildings, antiques, jewelry, art, and top notch gadgets! Florian has some of the coolest things in Second Life! Many of the gadgets are used through out Sheffield, so look for them and test them before you buy them! Oh, and Florian is a super nice fellow - make sure you drop by and say "hi" to him!


Olivia Rassir 's The Swanky Inkwell Tattoos and Accessories

Olivia's quaint shop contains all types of tattoos, from the classy to the naughty. Tattoos can be worn on layers under your clothes and come in all types of styles and include all types of subject matter. Plus, check out her accessories, that include body piercing, hats, purses, and other cool things. All at very affordable prices!


Mirelle Linette's Mirelle's Fantastic Magic Shop!

Makers of fine stage illusions! Magic tricks for the amateur and professional magician. At Mirelle's shop, you will find : magic tricks, illusions, special effects, prestidigitation, stage magic, the sawing in half illusion, and many, many more cool tricks!
