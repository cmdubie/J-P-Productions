The Sheffield Business Shops offer a wide variety of storefronts to locate your place of business. The Sheffield Business buildings are wonderful pre-fab structures that have incredible textures and hyper realistic designs. Our apartments are themed for Sheffield's 1930's era, and yet modern enough for everyone's needs. Though we encourage you to sell era-themed products, anything can be sold in our shops, that follow the guidelines below.

We have buildings that have both one floor display areas, as well as two floor structures. (Same rent costs.) Access to second floors are convenient, using either stairs or teleport elevators. There are many designs to choose from as well. Each building is located in high traffic areas in town, surrounded by interactive attractions. (Like the popular Jack's Nightclub.) Most of our current renters have been with us for years!

Be sure to stop by and check out our wonderful places of business- first come first serve!


Rules and Regulations For Business Renters

Please read these basic rules. Failure to do so may result in terminating your rental with no refund and being banned from the sim. By renting from Zoltan Mint or any representative of Sheffield Estates, you are accepting the terms of this agreement.

Liability Waiver - As a renter of Sheffield Business properties, you agree not to hold Zoltan Mint and/or any representative of Sheffield Estates, liable for any loss or damage to any or all rezzed items/prims that may occur beyond their control. This covenant is updated regularly, and the most current version will supersede any previous versions.

Payment - You are required and expected to pay your rent on time, weekly. Please pay the individual Business Rent Box. (Just right of each business building's door.) You will receive an IM when your rent becomes due 24 hours in advance. If you are later than 24 hours in paying late rent, you will be evicted. You may pay for at least one week's rent or up to 4 weeks rent in advance. If someone other than yourself pays the rent, you will have to contact Zoltan Mint for clarification.

Current Rent for a Sheffield Business = 250 lindens a week for 50 prims.

Group Tag - Upon renting, you will be invited to the Minty Entertainment Group to have the ability to rezz and obtain a prim counter. Please IM Zoltan Mint upon renting. Please be aware that I am not online 24 hours a day. My messages are forwarded to my E-mail. You will be added within 24 hours.

Theme - Sheffield is a small sea port town, catering to folks interested in the Victorian, Steampunk, and Gas Light role playing eras. Sheffield welcomes visitors, business owners, gamers, fun seekers, and everyone to explore this cool place! Sheffield consists of a limited number of rent buildings. We do not offer parcels or land, but instead, we offer pre-fab building spaces. You are not allowed to rez your own house, or any large structure on the desired space. If you do, it will be returned to you with in 24 hours. Each building will have different set prim allotments. You may rez furniture and/or business items as you see fit, but please try to keep your rezzed items to as close as the theme as possible. (1900's - 1940's.) Ugly, garish items, either in your yard, business, and/or extended from your house will get a warning for removal. Please do not attempt to alter or delete any prefab prim items already existing in your rental parcel.

Permissible Products - Feel free to sell anything inside the buildings you are renting. However, Please do not sell any of the following items; griefing items or other things that can cause harm to sims, other avatars, or prims. Also, please no sex related items that contain under age persons, either real life or Second Life in nature. If you are selling sexually explicit material, please be tasteful and do not rez out pictures of sexual acts, either real or pixel. Please try to keep to the them of this parcel - 1900's - 1940's in both products and decorations. Thank you.

About Vendors - You only have 50 prims to display your wares. Our recommendation is to use the available ONE PRIM vendors that are found on Marketplace or in shops in-world. Try to find low prim displays and vendors that are running 1-2 scripts. There are many and inexpensive too.

Lag - To keep this sim as a low-lag sim, please keep your business locations to 20 people per business, per hour. When lag becomes unusually severe, we will res-set the sim, so be prepared to move to another location when this occurs. Re-sets last approximately 2 minutes.

Prims - Please do not go over your allotted prim limit. To check how many prims you have rezzed, please check your prim box, which will keep track of everything you bring into the parcel.(The prim box is the small green box under your rent box.) If you go over your prim limit, you will be warned. If you do not reduce the prim count within 24 hours, the Sheffield owners will return items to you that they see fit. MAKE SURE YOU ARE WEARING THE GROUP TAG WHEN REZZING! Anything rezzed under the wrong tag will be returned after 5 minutes. Continuous problems with the above will get your rental terminated with no refund.

Scripts - Please keep scripts to a minimum. The Sheffield owners reserve the right to return any item that is script heavy and affecting the sim. This includes scripts that you wear on your person. Business owners are encouraged to us low memory vendor script to encourage a lag free sim. Single vendors with one script functions are the best.

Media - The parcel uses theme-appropriate music and media that can not be changed by renters. If we have enough requests, we can change the streams and/or media. Feel free to make suggestions. Note that the stream will be changed to individual performer's streams on the night of live entertainment at Jack's Nightclub.

Neighbors - Please be courteous of your neighbors. This is a beautiful sim and we attract high-end clientele that appreciate fine living in a lag-free and privacy initiated sim. As in real life, please show each other respect. If you choose to use a security system, do not set the range to anything over 10 meters. This will encroach on your neighbors property if set higher. Also, please make sure you are not encroaching your prims into other business space. No use of poofers, particle emitters, or large prims that traverse into another person's property please. Keep displays on the sidewalks in front of your store to a minimum. You may rez on the sidewalks, But nothing on the streets please!

Adult Activities (sex) - Since these are private businesses on a mature/moderate sim adult activities are allowed INSIDE only. Public adult activities are not allowed. Any activity involving age play (children avatars, etc.) will not be tolerated. This is against the SL terms of service. You will be banned and reported to SL immediately.

Renewing - If you choose not to renew rent, please let Zoltan Mint know a week in advance if possible. We have a waiting list or folks trying to get your spot! If you do not pay your rent within 24 hours of the due date, you will be ejected from the group and your items returned.

Griefing - Anything deemed griefing is strictly prohibited. This includes weapons, griefer HUDS, caging, spying on neighbors, trespassing on neighbors property, harassing in chat or IM and the like. If you are caught or reported for griefing, you will be banned and not refunded.

We thank you for joining our family of renters in Sheffield. Please explore the parcel and you will find everything you need in SL is right here! Enjoy your stay!!

Please contact Zoltan Mint with any additional questions.


How To Rent Space In Sheffield:

Simply pay the green rent box to the right of the building's entrance. You can pay for 1- 4 weeks in advance if you desire. With-in 24 hours, (if not sooner), a Land Manager will contact you and give you the sim's Group Tag, allowing you to rez what ever you need to rez in your space. The small green box under the rent box will keep track of your rezzed prims. Click on it to find what prims you have rezzed already.
