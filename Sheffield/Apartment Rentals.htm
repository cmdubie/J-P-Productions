The Sheffield Apartments are small efficiency living spaces that are suitable for up to two people. Each apartment is fully furnished and you can temporarily rez boxes and other small items. Each apartment has its own teleporter and each apartment's walls can be changed via an easy to use texture changer that exist near the front door. There is also an on/off front door light and lockable doors (Type "show help" on local chat for instructions.)

Each apartment comes furnished with a modifiable living room set that you can add or subtract parts. You can also change the color and the chairs have multiple animations for singles and couples. You also receive a dining room set that you can change wood and table cloth color. We include a bookshelf that contains many books that you can actually read via delivered note cards Finally, we include a very good bed that can be texture changed as well. The bed contains over 500 animations for couples, singles, and more!

Our apartments are both themed for Sheffield's 1930's era, but yet modern enough for everyone's needs. Be sure to stop by and check them out - first come first serve!

Please read these basic rules. Failure to do so may result in terminating your rental with no refund. By renting from Zoltan Mint or any representative of Sheffield Estates, you are accepting the terms of this agreement.

Liability Waiver - As a renter and/or visitor of Sheffield Estate properties, you agree not to hold Zoltan Mint and/or any representative of Sheffield Estates, liable for any loss or damage to any or all rezzed items/prims that may occur beyond their control. This covenant is updated regularly, and the most current version will supersede any previous versions.

Payment - You are required and expected to pay your rent on time, weekly. Please pay the individual Apartment rent box. (The one on the porch of the floor you desire.) You will receive an IMl when your rent becomes due 24 hours in advance. If you are later than 24 hours paying late rent, you will be evicted. You may pay for at least one week's rent or up to 4 weeks rent in advance. If someone other than your or your living companion pays the rent, you will have to contact Zoltan Mint for clarification.

Current Rent for a Sheffield Apartment = 150 lindens a week.

Group Tag - Upon renting, you will be invited to the Sheffield Estate Group to have the ability to rezz. Please IM Zoltan Mint upon renting. Please be aware that I am not online 24 hours a day. My messages are forwarded to my E-mail. You will be added within 24 hours.

Theme - Sheffield is a small sea port town, catering to folks interested in the Victorian, Steampunk, and Gas Light role playing eras. Sheffield welcomes visitors, business owners, gamers, fun seekers, and everyone to explore this cool place! Sheffield consists of a limited number of rent buildings. We do not offer parcels or land, but instead, we offer pre-fab building spaces. Please do not attempt to alter or delete any prefab prim items already existing in your rental parcel.

Lag - To keep this sim as a low-lag sim, please keep your guest count in private homes to less than 5 avatars. When lag becomes unusually severe, we will res-set the sim, so be prepared to move to another location. Re-sets last approximately 2 minutes.

Scripts - Please keep scripts to a minimum. The Sheffield owners reserve the right to return any item that is script heavy and affecting the sim. This includes scripts that you wear on your person. We don not recommend rezzing high scripted items like pets.

Media - The parcel uses theme-appropriate music and media that can not be changed by renters. If we have enough requests, we can change the streams and/or media. Feel free to make suggestions. Note that the stream will be changed to individual performer's streams on the night of live entertainment at Jack's Nightclub.

Guests - Upon renting you can have one other person added to the group to rezz and live on your rented property. Their prims count towards your total prim limit. If you need more than one person added, please IM Zoltan Mint. It is to his discretion, and it is not guaranteed. This sim is rezz enabled with a 5 minute return limit. So if you just need someone to rezz purchases to unpack, they can without the group tag. You are responsible for your guests actions. Please explain to your guest that they too, will need to wear their group tag while rezzing on this sim.

Neighbors - Please be courteous of your neighbors. This is a beautiful sim and we attract high-end clientele that appreciate fine living in a lag-free and privacy initiated sim. As in real life, please show each other respect. If you choose to use a security system, do not set the range to anything over 10 meters. This will encroach on your neighbors property if set higher. Also, please make sure you are not encroaching your prims into other apartment dweller's space. No use of poofers, particle emitters, or large prims that traverse into another person's property please.

Adult Activities (sex) - Since these are private residences on a mature/moderate sim adult activities are allowed INSIDE only. Public adult activities are not allowed. Any activity involving age play (children avatars, etc.) will not be tolerated. This is against the SL terms of service. You will be banned and reported to SL immediately.

Renewing - If you choose not to renew rent, please let Zoltan Mint know a week in advance if possible. We have a waiting list or folks trying to get your spot! If you do not pay your rent within 24 hours of the due date, you will be ejected from the group and your items returned.

Griefing - Anything deemed griefing is strictly prohibited. This includes weapons, griefer HUDS, caging, spying on neighbors, trespassing on neighbors property, harassing in chat or IM and the like. If you are caught or reported for griefing, you will be banned and not refunded.

We thank you for joining our family of renters in Sheffield. Please explore the parcel and you will find everything you need in SL is right here! Enjoy your stay!!
