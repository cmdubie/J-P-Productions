Jack's is a cool and hip nightclub in Second Life that was chosen by the Linden Lab's Editors as a "Recommended Destination." We are a live entertainment venue that features singers, musicians, comedians, magicians, parties, live DJs, contests, and all types of fun and games nightly. We have 3 floors of excitement in an awesome industrial grunge warehouse! All we want is for you to have fun!

Zoltan Mint opened Jack's Nightclub in November of 2010. Named after the club's Boston Terrier mascot, Jack's was created to showcase live entertainers and theme parties. Zoltan is a SL (and RL) Performer and Manager, so he appreciates the value of quality entertainment. He performs magic and comedy all around Second Life as well, including a monthly magic show at Jacks!

We have been featured in "Second Nighter's Magazine," as well as other popular Second Life publications and we are frequently "trended" on the Linden Lab's Event's Forums.

Jack's Nightclub features live entertainment weekly! We have live comedians, musicians, singers, magicians, story readers, and all other types of acts! See our schedule in the club or here for all the latest updates of live entertainment! We have two stages inside Jack's Nightclub, as well as other stages through out our sim! There is a large venue stage, and a smaller, more intimate stage for smaller acts on the third floor.

We have frequent theme party nights! Special nights include Neko Nights, Formal Wear Nights, Lingerie Nights, Super Hero Nights, Classic Rock Nights, and other costume fun!

Jack's also has great party music for getting your groove on or to just chill. Our dance animation balls are state-of-the-art and include some of the best dances in Second Life! You will find couple's dances in the floating blue ball above the dance floor and single dances in the dance machine on the wall near the entrance.

We have great interactive games too! Trivia, Bingo, Mud Wrestling, Truthball, a Splodder, and many social party games. Plus spontaneous fun like snowball fights and other crazy activities makes Jack's the fun and premier spot to hang out!

There are always contests going on too! We have many costume contests, raffles, lotteries, photo contests, and many more fun and exciting contests for cash prizes!

You can also rent the club for any private function, like private parties, weddings, receptions, or rez day gatherings! Speak with a manager for rates and more information!

Jack's is simply the most fun you can have in one nightclub! All are welcome! Come down and start having some fun now!

Jack's Nightclub's Patron Survival Guide

New to Jack's Nightclub? Or are you a regular that may be overwhelmed with all that Jack's has to offer? This Survival Guide will give you hints and tips on how to make your experience at Jack's Nightclub the best it can be.

1) First and foremost, relax and have a good time! Jack's was designed for your entertainment and relaxation. We want your experience here to be fun and trouble free. Read through the tips below and if you have any other questions, just ask a Manager or Host- we are here to make you happy!

2) Turn your day settings to "Midnight!" Jack's stage looks best in the dark, specially the lighting effects on the main stage!

3) If Jack's is running a contest and you want in the contest - enter the contest board to the right of the stage on the main floor. Just click "Enter" and you are in! When voting starts, you can vote for one person, but not for yourself. The contestant with the most votes after a set period of time, wins the prize listed on board! Please vote when you see the contest board is open for voting. (You usually get around 15 minutes to vote.) You have to stick around to win however, so keep watching local chat to see your name!

4) Make sure you explore all 3 floors! We have contests, games, live entertainment, dance areas, on all floors and on outside porches too! You have full access to all the doors and rooms on all the floors!

5) There is so much to do here that you may miss some things! Here is a list of most of the things we offer at Jack's Nightclub:

- Play Trivia - 2nd floor back patio - held on the first Thursday of each month.
- Play Truth Ball - 2nd floor - with many expansions available.
- Play Bingo - First Monday of each month! Come play for cash! Lots of fun! First floor patio.
- Mud Wrestle - 3rd floor outside roof - free muddy bathing suits available!
- Contest Board - 1st floor, just left of the stage - check board for current contest.
- Live shows! Ground Floor Main Stage (Stage One), Third Floor Stages (Stage Two).

6) We are delighted you are here and hope your stay is as pleasurable as possible. Friendly reminder: We are a "mature" rated club residing on a mature rated sim. Please, NO NUDITY, ESCORTS, WEAPONS, OBSCENE LANGUAGE, SOLICITATIONS, or SPAM! Also, please do not voice while there are live entertainers. Please adjust your avatar and behavior accordingly. Thank you for cooperating with this club policy!

7) Feel free to teleport your friends in here! The more the merrier! TP them directly and they will not have to use the front door.

8) Please help us keep Jack's Nightclub open! Donate some Lindens to any of the donation boxes. (Main donation box is in front of the Main Stage on the ground floor.) Thank you! Also - please do not forget to tip the Performers!

9) Please join the Jack's Nightclub Group! We will keep you informed about all the upcoming events and send a limo to pick you up on the night of each event! (We do not spam!) To join, click on any tip box (you do not have to make a donation) and select "Join Group." The group invite will appear in local chat. You can also private IM any of our staff for an invite too!

10) Check the message board near the front entrance and the event board on the back wall. The Event Board scrolls though the next few months of events. You can also click on each event's question mark for additional information about each event. The message board highlights all the big events that are currently happening.

11) If there is a live performer at Jack's, we ask you to keep local chatter to a minimum. Please use private IMs to communicate personal matters. And please do not voice during shows. Feel free to use applause and laugh gestures. It will show your avi is enjoying the show in local.

12) All of the seats, boards, bean bags, and crates have multiple sitting positions in them. Right click on them and choose sit. If you want to change your position, simple click on the seat for a sit menu.

13) The dance ball is near the ceiling on the ground floor. This is a state-of-the-art dance ball that contains couples dance animations. You can choose many different dance styles from sub-menus that will appear on your screen when you click on the ball. You will need to accept the animation request and turn your AO (Animation Over-ride) off for the dances to run smoothly. Also, please be patient, SL lag may slow the animations from getting to you.

14) For single dances, use the Dance Machine on the wall near the front entrance. Select from the blue male or pink female dancer in the picture. There are many cool dances inside that span all the dance styles out there. Say "yes" when the machine asks to animate your avatar, and turn off your AO.

15) Feeling Lucky? Jack's has a "splodder" near the ceiling on the ground floor. Just left click on it and pay the amount you want to gamble. (Minimum of 10 L). The more you pay, the better your chances of winning a bigger pot. The splodder needs 5 people to play. As soon as 5 have paid, it will count downs and explode with money! Everyone who plays will receive something. Try it! Its a blast!

16) Want to know who has played here in the past? Take as look at our scrolling picture viewer near the bar. There you will find all the wonderful talent that has graced our stages in the past!

17) Make sure you look for all the special effects on the stage during live events. We have many colored lights, smoke, fireworks, explosions, and fire during live shows!

18) Jack's throws some crazy theme parties! Everything from lingerie and beach parties, to formal dress and holiday parties! Our Neko party is legendary! We have costume contests and events at each of these parties. If you need a costume for these events, there is a costume shop down the road from Jack's in Sheffield.

19) Performers and special VIPS are invited to hang out behind the stage in Jack's "Green Room!" There you will find comfy couches to sit on and wait for your gig, as well as a bottle of champagne to imbibe while you relax. We really do appreciate our performer's talents!

20) Our last hint? Relax! We here at Jack's believe that SL was created to have fun and escape RL stress. We are very informal here and fun loving types. We get pretty crazy at times and want you to get crazy with us. Become a regular and you will find long-lasting relationships will form. Join us everyday! We love to party!!!

Thanks and we hope these tips help you have a better time here! If you have any questions, IM a Manager or Host. Enjoy yourself!


Jack's Nightclub's Performer Survival Guide

Hello! And thanks for performing at Jack's Nightclub - Second Life's premier live entertainment location! Here are some hints that will make your performance here much easier for you! If you have any questions, please Instant Message Zoltan Mint.


YOUR STREAM
We do not use a Shoutcast board. Because things change so often in SL, (namely your streams), we found it easier to just add your stream manually. We have your current stream in your profile notes. So if it changes, just let us know. On show night, we will plug your stream in 1-5 minutes before the show. Please make sure your stream is working properly and you are broadcasting from your end. You may plug your stream in yourself if a Manager is not available.


YOUR TIP BOARD (And other props)
We recommend that you rez your tip board and other props on the ground next to the stage, or at least on the edge of the stage. The reason is, our stage has nicely designed lighting effects, and those lights have "prim beams." These beams may slightly interfere with guests trying to click on your items. Generally, if they zoom in on your props, there is no problem. This is not a big issue, just something to be aware of if you have lots of rezzed items. Oh yeah, the microphone on stage can be lowered by clicking its base and raised by clicking the mic piece. Adjust it to your avi's height appropriately.

YOUR FANS
Please send out as many notifications you can to any and all of your groups. We will send notifications out also, including pay groups that contain over 10,000 members.

YOUR LIGHTS
Someone will be running lights and effects on the stage for you. Don't worry, these lights are very professional and subtle and will add entertainment value to the show. If you have any special request - like songs that you want smoke, fire or explosions, please tell us in advance. Otherwise, we will do our best to make them awesome.


YOUR TIME
You are welcome to perform over your scheduled hour time slot, if there is no one immediately following you. If there is someone following you, please finish a few minutes early, including cleaning up your rezzed props. Otherwise. Please feel free to sing a few more songs and take an encore! The updated schedule is on the wall near the front entrance.


YOUR PERFORMANCE
We truly love live performers here at Jack's. The proof? We send out notifications to over 20 groups at a cost of over 200L a show. Plus, we have hired a Hostess for you, listed your show in LL's Events, and scheduled your show on our Google Public Calendar. If you encourage your fans to explore our sim and donate to the venue (tip box in front of stage), this will help us keep Jack's open.

Thank you again for being so awesome! Please "break-a-leg," and have a great and profitable performance!


Why Jack's Nightclub Does Not Pay Performers

As many of you know, we have consciously made the decision to not offer lindens to entertainers who perform at Jack's Nightclub. Please understand that this decision was made after 3 years of experience while keeping Jack's Nightclub open in Second Life. Also we have had many conversations with other performance venue owners, and many chats with live performers in Second Life, including full time professionals, and casual new entertainers. Please understand that this is not meant as an insult or to question anyone's skill as a performer. It is simply our decision on how we need to do business in SL. In the light of this, here is why we made this decision.

The first and biggest reason is that venues suffer the biggest blow in costs. Venue owners must pay extravagant tier costs weekly. The average rent is around 5500 lindens a week for a 1/4 sized parcel. Then there is the cost of the buildings, gadgets, furniture, and other amenities that make the place attractive and inviting. Plus we at Jack's Nightclub spend close to 200L per show on advertising each performer's show, to attract fans and get the word out to the entire grid. Throw in other incidental costs, and you have a very expensive endeavor to produce every night of the week.

A performer has expenses too, and we realize this. However, it is no where near the expense a venue owner has. Spending the one time cost for instrument prims, getting a publicity photo, and paying for the competitively low priced music streams are minimal. These expenditures can be matched in a single show with tips. A venue owner must pay the weekly tier or will be forced to close down. And we all know that happens way too often with venues in Second Life.

We also understand that most performers are professional and worthy of venue payment. Unfortunately, most performers are not doing the math correctly. If we were to pay all our performers 5000L each, for each show (5000L seems to be the average asking price for experienced singers), then we would be shilling out over 70,000L a week for entertainers. That is over $300 a week in real life cash. That is much more than tickets for a national touring band! Add the other costs and you will quickly see that it simply is not a reasonable business practice, at least for us. I know of no entertainment venue that could stay open very long with those costs.

If you are a performer demanding venue pay - ask yourself this. If 5000L (or less) is that important to you in Second Life, why not play out in the real world and get more money? After all, 5000L is around $20. Certainly, you will make more in the real world. Why not use Second Life to reach new fans, network with other musicians and promoters, practice your craft, and just have fun? Remember fun? That was what Second Life was created for!

Second reason we don't pay - your fans should be the ones supporting you, not venues that give you the forum to perform in. That is a hard pill to swallow, but true. Grow your fan base by being professional, polite, entertaining and talented. They will come to see you play, and often. You should have a fan group with no less than 200 people. Send notifications out for every show. Your fans will follow you, tip you, and you will certainly make enough money to cover your expenses. On average, the bigger performers that play at Jack's Nightclub make between 1000 and 5000L in tips anyway. That is the true test of popularity - having fans support your endeavor on Second Life. By the way, many large venues in real life are paid by the performers to rent the hall. "Four walling" in Vegas is now the norm for most entertainers. Why then, should it be different in Second Life?

Finally, we at Jacks Nightclub truly love singers, musicians and other types of live entertainment. We are respectful and polite to all our performers. Most become good friends. There is a reason we are a Linden Lab's Editor Pick for 3 years in a row. There is a reason that Jack's Nightclub is frequently "trending" on the forums. There is a reason why most big, professional acts PAY US, after their performance. It is because we understand that music, and all entertainment on Second Life should be fun, exciting, and entertaining. If it becomes a question of money demands, then venues will continue to just disappear and performers will struggle to find places that are open and accepting live acts.

I am a live performer in Second Life. I have purchased thousands of lindens worth of props. I have spent hours writing jokes and scripts. After 3 years, I still do not require the venues to pay me. Tips are just fine. And I am having a blast!

Re-look your needs. How about doing what you love with less desire for gain? You may just find yourself with a huge following, filled up gig calendars, and friends with some true, honest, and respectful people.

Let's entertain and have fun!
