You’re Guide to Ferndale

Welcome to the wonderful world of Ferndale! We have created this place for you to work and play in! This guide will help you understand everything there is to do here!

Ferndale is a small sea port town, catering to folks interested in the fantasy and medieval role playing eras. Ferndale welcomes visitors, business owners, gamers, fun seekers, and everyone to explore this cool place!

Every main location in Ferndale is equipped with state-of-the-art "gadgets" to make your visit easier and more fun. Look for “Giver” signs located throughout the sim. These will give you land marks when you click on them, important note cards (like this one!) and some even dispense free gifts! And please remember our tip boxes! It took a lot of blood, sweat and tears, (and cash) to create this place. Help support it by a kind donation!

Please observe the rules in the Parcel Covenant. They are simple and are enforced by the land holders (Larimore Estates) for everyone’s safety and enjoyment of this wonderful place. Now on to the Attractions!

The sim has the following attractions:

-- The Bearded Dragon Inn - A popular watering hole as well as great kitchen for all your culinary needs. The Inn also offers inexpensive beds upstairs for rest and relaxation.

--The Blacksmith - Stop by and say hello to our friendly metal worker They make all types of weapons, armor, and even the occasional horse shoe!

--Castle Ferndale - This old edifice is the seat of power of Ferndale. Here you will find a great library, meeting halls, and other great rooms of knowledge.

--The Temple - You will find more than worship here. This community chapel also is the residence of the town healer.

-- The Magic Shop - All types of trinkets and magical gizmos can be found here in this little treasure trove! Visit, but be aware - the owner would much rather you not touch his talismans!

--The Miller - Here you will find our town miller. All types of grains are ground here by the huge grinding wheels, fed by our lovely river. Stop by for a sample of their wonderful rye and oats.

So there you go! So much to do, so little time! Grab yourself a cold drink, turn off the lights in your room, lock the door, take the phone off the hook, and immerse yourself in the world of Ferndale. Forget about the real world for a little while. That was our goal!

Happy Travels!


About Role Playing in the Town of Ferndale

Everyone is welcome to role play in the small medieval town of Ferndale. You will find all the amenities to make your role playing experience fun and exciting!

Rules and Regulations

- Please abide by both the Larimore Estate and Ferndale Parcel's Covenant. You will find the covenants in-world under "World/About Land/Covenant." Read them, understand them and there will be no problems!

- The Ferndale sim is open to all, for both "open play" role playing, as well as scheduled, Game Master ran events. If there is a conflict of availability of space or time, the scheduled games will have priority. Please respect these groups and leave them alone. There are many scheduled games available for you to play in here. Just ask the Game Masters when and where they get together!

- The Ferndale sim has been designed for any of the following role play scenarios:

- Medieval era
- Dungeons & Dragons
- Gorean (No slavery/explicit sex content)
- Renaissance era
- Piazo's Pathfinder Adventures
- Conan and other fictional literature
- Game of Thrones stories
- Any sword and sorcery RPG settings

- Please respect the eras above and dress, role play, and act accordingly. There are many fine shops both in Ferndale and around the grid that sell appropriate clothing and gear. Try to "play the part" and bring these exciting and adventurous times to life here on this sim! Ferndale was deliberately created to be some what "generic," to allow any game to play here.

- Absolutely no pedophilia, griefing, spamming, too many attached scripts, open nudity, open sexual acts, excessive swearing, sexual solicitations, and anything else that would disrupt in other's fun.



- The Damage function may be turned on occasionally. You will know when it is on when you see a small heart on the top of your viewer's screen. Please IM Zoltan Mint if you desire this feature turned on.

- Please get permission from Zoltan Mint if you wish to implement the use of combat HUDs. Though we do not mind these HUDs, we would like to know when they are being used.

- Please refrain from flying, teleporting, and camming while here. (These items are not turned off, but by not doing them, you are preserving the atmosphere!)

- Please do not bother the shoppers or shop owners in any of the commercial shops in Ferndale. Further more, please stay out of the private homes. Those are private residencies that can be rented if desired.

- Please donate what you can. If you like this place and use it to play in, your donations will help keep it open!

- Have fun and play nice! This is what Second Life was meant for!


Rules and Regulations For Business Renters

Please read these basic rules. Failure to do so may result in terminating your rental with no refund and being banned from the sim. By renting from Zoltan Mint or any representative of Ferndale Estates, you are accepting the terms of this agreement.

The Ferndale Business buildings are wonderful pre-fab structures that have incredible textures and hyper realistic designs. Business owners are encouraged to adapt to the theme of this parcel to keep things consistent. (See "Theme" below.) However, renters can sell what ever they desire, as long as it follows the guidelines below. (See "Permissible Products," below.)

Liability Waiver - As a renter and/or visitor of Ferndale Estate properties, you agree not to hold Zoltan Mint and/or any representative of Ferndale Estates, liable for any loss or damage to any or all rezzed items/prims that may occur beyond their control. This covenant is updated regularly, and the most current version will supersede any previous versions.

Payment - You are required and expected to pay your rent on time, weekly. Please pay the individual Business Rent Box. (Just right of each business building's door.) You will receive an IM/E-Mail when your rent becomes due 24 hours in advance. If you are later than 24 hours in paying late rent, you will be evicted. You may pay for at least one week's rent or up to 4 weeks rent in advance. If someone other than yourself or your living companion pays the rent, you will have to contact Zoltan Mint for clarification.

Current Rent for a Ferndale Business = 250 lindens a week for 50 prims.

Group Tag - Upon renting, you will be invited to the Mint Entertainment Group to have the ability to rezz and obtain a prim counter. Please IM Zoltan Mint upon renting. Please be aware that I am not online 24 hours a day. My messages are forwarded to my E-mail. You will be added within 24 hours.

Theme - Ferndale is a small medieval town, catering to folks interested in the medieval, fantasy or renaissance role playing eras. Ferndale welcomes visitors, business owners, gamers, fun seekers, and everyone to explore this cool place! Ferndale consists of a limited number of rent buildings. We do not offer parcels or land, but instead, we offer pre-fab building spaces. You are not allowed to rez your own house, or any large structure on the desired space. If you do, it will be returned to you with in 24 hours. Each building will have different set prim allotments. You may rez furniture and/or business items as you see fit, but please try to keep your rezzed items to as close as the theme as possible. (Medieval) Ugly, garish items, either in your yard, business, and/or extended from your house will get a warning for removal. Please do not attempt to alter or delete any prefab prim items already existing in your rental parcel.

Permissible Products - Feel free to sell anything inside the buildings you are renting. However, Please do not sell any of the following items; griefing items or other things that can cause harm to sims, other avatars, or prims. Also, please no sex related items that contain under age persons, either real life or Second Life in nature. If you are selling sexually explicit material, please be tasteful and do not rez out pictures of sexual acts, either real or pixel. Please try to keep to the theme of this parcel - Medieval, in both products and decorations. Thank you.

About Vendors - You only have 50 prims to display your wares. Our recommendation is to use the available ONE PRIM vendors that are found on Marketstreet or in shops in-world. Try to find low prim displays and vendors that are running 1-2 scripts. There are many and inexpensive too.

Lag - To keep this sim as a low-lag sim, please keep your guest count in private homes to less than 5 avatars. Business locations will have a limit of 20 people per business, per hour. When lag becomes unusually severe, we will res-set the sim, so be prepared to move to another location when this occurs. Re-sets last approximately 2 minutes.

Prims - Please do not go over your allocated prim limit. To check how many prims you have rezzed, please check your rent box, which will keep track of everything you bring into the parcel. If you go over your prim limit, you will be warned. If you do not reduce the prim count within 24 hours, the Ferndale owners will return items to you that they see fit. MAKE SURE YOU ARE WEARING THE GROUP TAG WHEN REZZING! Anything rezzed under the wrong tag will be returned after 5 minutes. Continuous problems with the above will get your rental terminated with no refund.

Scripts - Please keep scripts to a minimum. The Ferndale owners reserve the right to return any item that is script heavy and affecting the sim. This includes scripts that you wear on your person. Business owners are encouraged to us low memory vendor script to encourage a lag free sim. Single vendors with one script functions are the best.

Media - The parcel uses theme-appropriate music and media that can not be changed by renters. If we have enough requests, we can change the streams and/or media. Feel free to make suggestions. Note that the stream will be changed to individual performer's streams on the night of live entertainment at Jack's Nightclub.

Neighbors - Please be courteous of your neighbors. This is a beautiful sim and we attract nice people who appreciate fine living in a lag-free and privacy initiated sim. As in real life, please show each other respect. If you choose to use a security system, do not set the range to anything over 10m. This will encroach on your neighbors property if set higher. Also, please make sure you are not encroaching your prims into other business space. No use of poofers, particle emitters, or large prims that traverse into another person's property please. Keep displays on the sidewalks in front of your store to a minimum. (Nothing on the streets please!)

Adult Activities (sex) - Since these are private residences on a mature/moderate sim adult activities are allowed INSIDE only. Public adult activities are not allowed. Any activity involving age play (children avatars, etc.) will not be tolerated. This is against the SL terms of service. You will be banned and reported to SL immediately.

Renewing - If you choose not to renew rent, please let Zoltan Mint know a week in advance if possible. We have a waiting list or folks trying to get your spot! If you do not pay your rent within 24 hours of the due date, you will be ejected from the group and your items returned.

Griefing - Anything deemed griefing is strictly prohibited. This includes weapons, griefer HUDS, caging, spying on neighbors, trespassing on neighbors property, harassing in chat or IM and the like. If you are caught or reported for griefing, you will be banned and not refunded.

We thank you for joining our family of renters in Ferndale. Please explore the parcel and you will find everything you need in SL is right here! Enjoy your stay!!
