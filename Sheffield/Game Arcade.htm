The Sheffield Arcade is a great place to hang out and engage in all types of competitive and social games! The Arcade houses both the Minty Goodness Game store, as well as Minty Posters and a whole bunch of free games to play with friends!

FIRST FLOOR

Minty Goodness Games sells many different games that are great for social interaction, fun get togethers, and party ice breakers. They have been designed for group use and come with many available expansions. These games are awesome for parties and clubs!

Click Here for the Minty Goodness Games Store on Marketplace!

You can test out and play all the Minty Games on the first floor of the Arcade. Invite some friends there, have a seat, and play any of the games on display for free! All the displayed games contain all the expansions as well!

Don't forget to talk to the floating robot. He has an artificial inelegance and can carry on a meaningful conversation!

SECOND FLOOR

The second floor houses Minty Goodness Posters, which are on display for sale. You will also find a few games to play with your friends, as well as a functioning photo booth!

Minty Goodness Posters are awesome one prim wall hangings that look great in clubs or homes. They are funny, thoughtful, and very topical!

There are some really fun games to play on this floor as well. Look for the Skeeball machine, the air hockey table, and the very addictive Zyngo, a game where you can win some serious lindens!

THIRD FLOOR

The floor, (accessible by use of the wall teleporter), has the feel of a private gambling club, but is open to all! You will find all types of games of chance and party fun! Keep in mind that gambling is not allowed in Second Life, so the games here are all for fun play only.

You will find a Texas Holdem poker table, a Roulette table, and there is even a Pictionary game board! And don't forget to play the very addictive "Greedy Greedy!" The drinks at the bar are free, so relax and enjoy yourself!
