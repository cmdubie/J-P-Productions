You’re Guide to the Tempus Space Station

Welcome to the Tempus Space Station! Here you will find everything you need to work, live and play in deep space! The Tempus is a huge place, with many levels and decks. It is so big, in fact, that you may get lost quickly. Let the following be your guide, and hopefully you can explore all the locations within the space station and have a great visit!

Note - To get around to all of the location listed below, use the highly advanced transport tube that you will find on each level. To use the transporter, just left click on the white circle in the transporter and a menu of locations will appear. Use this menu and click the corresponding button to the location you wish to travel to. Wait till the white floor turns green, then left click the green area and you will immediately teleport to your new destination.

COMMAND CENTER
This is the heart of the space station. Here you will find controls for the communication systems, alert systems, navigation, main controls, and even an artificial intelligence named "Isis." The captain of the ship commands all operations from this location.
Note - most of the controls are limited to Captains or crew that are wearing the "Mint Entertainment" tag.

AIRLOCKS
This controlled area will open a door to open space. Used primarily to vent junk, it could be used to extract enemies. The control station here will open or close both doors.

AUXILIARY
These small levels are mostly used for storage. Most station supplies are brought and cataloged here. These areas are sometimes used for small meetings and training.

DOCKING BAY AND DOCKING ARM
This level contains the large ship docking arm and controls. The arm can adapt to any ship that wishes to dock at the Tempus. There are two control stations - one on the docking bay level, and a second near the end of the docking arm.

ENGINE ROOM
This area consists of three levels that surround the engine core. They are primarily used by engineering only and are dangerous places for the casual visitor.

HANGER
This large space is used to house the smaller ships like the transports and fighters. A large door with a force field allows ships to come and go as needed. Inside you will find several ships to fly. You will have to get Zoltan Mint to "unlock" them for you. Once unlocked, you left click them and "pilot" or sit in them. You can then fly them around space - either for fun or for dogfights!

 

LANDING PADS
Three landing pads provide areas where small to medium ships can land and lock down. A center elevator allows space travelers to safely rise out onto the pads.

LEVEL ONE
This is the largest and most busiest level on the space station. This area contains all the day-to-day operation rooms to keep the station running. You will find the following areas (most of which are restricted) on Level One:

Main Concourse - A huge space viewing area and running track.
The Head - Bathrooms and public showers.
The Infirmary - Emergancy treatment and hospital.
Meeting Room - Three tables and remote control centers.
Weapons/Armor Supply - lockers full of weapons and space armor.
Military Bunks - This large facility houses the station's men at arms.
Cryo-Chamber - This area is for long term travel - sleep chambers for any length of time.
Astrophysics - This small area is where courses are plotted and the Main Frame is monitored.

LEVEL TWO
This level mostly consists of suites for the officers and guests. Also, electronic and engineering have workrooms on this level. Access to the station's service conduits are available on this level.

OBSERVATORY
This level is for fun and relaxation. Travelers and crew will find a dance club here, as well as a bar and replicator for food and drink. There is a small cafe and electronic games that are free to use and play. Also, the wide windows allows the best view of space!


About Role Playing on the Tempus Space Station

Everyone is welcome to role play on the Tempus Space Station. You will find all the amenities to make your role playing experience fun and exciting!

Rules and Regulations

- Please abide by both the Larimore Estate and Sheffield Parcel's Covenant. You will find the covenants in-world under "World/About Land/Covenant." Read them, understand them and there will be no problems!

- The Space Station is open to all, for both "open play" role playing, as well as scheduled, Game Master ran events. If there is a conflict of availability of space or time, the scheduled games will have priority. Please respect these groups and leave them alone. There are many scheduled games available for you to play in here. Just ask the Game Masters when and where they get together!

- The Space Station has been designed for any of the following role play scenarios:

- Sci- Fi RPGs
- Alien movie RPGs
- Star Wars RPGs
- Star Trek RPGs
- Near Future Space Adventures
- Firefly RPGs
- Super Hero RPGs
- Any other settings

- Please respect the eras above and dress, role play, and act accordingly. There are many fine shops around the grid that sell appropriate clothing and gear. Try to "play the part" and bring these exciting and adventurous times to life here on this sim! The Tempus was deliberately created to be some what "generic," to allow any game to play here.

- Absolutely no pedophilia, griefing, spamming, too many attached scripts, open nudity, open sexual acts, excessive swearing, sexual solicitations, and anything else that would disrupt in other's fun.

- The Damage function may be turned on occasionally. You will know when it is on when you see a small heart on the top of your viewer's screen. Please IM Zoltan Mint if you desire this feature turned on.

- Please get permission from Zoltan Mint if you wish to implement the use of combat HUDs. Though we do not mind these HUDs, we would like to know when they are being used.

- Please refrain from flying, teleporting, and camming while here. (These items are not turned off, but by not doing them, you are preserving the atmosphere!)

- Please do not bother the shoppers or shop owners in any of the commercial shops on the ground, under the Space Station. . Further more, please stay out of the private homes. Those are private residencies that can be rented if desired.

- Please donate what you can. If you like this place and use it to play in, your donations will help keep it open!

- Have fun and play nice! This is what Second Life was meant for!
