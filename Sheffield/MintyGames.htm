This or That

"This or That" is a fun, social party game and conversation starter. It was designed for two or more people and is specially fun with large groups. Play it at parties and other social events, or use it to get to know that new romantic interest! You can have hours of fun playing the game as is, or coming up with your own "house rules." It is simple to play, fast and fun, and you may learn something new about your friends, loved ones, and perhaps even yourself!

The original game comes loaded with three starter subjects - "General #1," "Movies #1," and "Dating #1." "General" is a sampling of mixed subjects. "Movies" covers old to modern cinema, and "Dating" is great for couples, specially new ones!

This or That is extremely easy to use. After purchase, rez the box and right click to open it. Copy the contents into your inventory. When you are ready to play it, drag the game out and position it to your taste. The game device can be sat on the floor or on a table or any surface you wish. Just make sure everyone can click on it.

Once out, (and your fellow players are situated and ready), click on the base of the game. This will bring up the Subject Card Menu. Only the Owner can click on the base to change the subject cards. Choose what subject you want to play. (Described above.) The game device will show you what subject is currently in play with floating text. You can change the subject as often as you wish. You may want to add expansion cards in the future. When you add them to you game device, they will show up in your menu too. You can create your own cards, or purchase pre-made cards and card sets from Minty Good Productions. (See below for contact information.)

After the subject is agreed upon, players may take turns right clicking on the upper game device. Every time someone clicks on the device, they will be given a random question, asking them to pick between two items. (This or That!) There is no score keeping, or winners in this game. You simply have fun picking your answers and then perhaps explaining why that is your choice. Other players should comment on the selections chosen too. Discuss and have fun! A lot of laughs can be had by comparing answers and discussing opinions on each question!

Play continues until the players are finished taking turns. There really is no "end" to the game. Questions are drawn randomly, so if a question repeats, simply ignore it and click the device for a new question. Be creative! Have fun!

Final Word!

"Final Word" is a fun, social party game and conversation starter. It was designed for two or more people and is specially fun with large groups. Play it at parties and other social events, or use it to get to know that new romantic interest! You can have hours of fun playing the game as is, or coming up with your own "house rules." It is simple to play, fast and fun, and you may learn something new about your friends, loved ones, and perhaps even yourself!

The original game comes loaded with two starter subjects - "General #1," and "Movies #1." Please visit the Minty Goodness In-World shop or on Marketplace for more card expansions! "General" is a sampling of mixed subjects. "Movies" covers all genres of film, including titles and actors!

Final Word is extremely easy to use. After purchase, rez the box and right click to open it. Copy the contents into your inventory. When you are ready to play it, drag the game out and position it to your taste. The game device can be sat on the floor or on a table or any surface you wish. Just make sure everyone can click on it.

Once out, (and your fellow players are situated and ready), click on the base of the game. This will bring up the Subject Card Menu. Only the Owner can click on the base to change the subject cards. Choose what subject you want to play. (Described above.) You can change the subject as often as you wish. You may want to add expansion Subject Cards in the future. When you add them to you game device, they will show up in your menu too. You can create your own cards, or purchase pre-made cards and card sets from Minty Good Games.

After the subject is agreed upon, players may take turns right clicking on the upper game device. Every time someone clicks on the device, they will be given a random category. Now everyone types into local chat, their answers. There is no need to worry about "turns" in this game, simply type in responses as they come to you. Everyone simply types in as many responses to the subject they can think of!

The last person who types in the "final word," or last response to any given subject, is considered the winner of that round. Repeat answers, of course, do not count. Repeat answers can be checked by having someone simply scroll up the local chat and locate the questioned answer. Some rounds can last a very long time if players have many responses. Only the Final Word in each category can win.

Hint - to avoid endless lists of words in certain categories, players may opt to allow a broad answer. Example: If the category is "Slasher Films," instead of listing every "Friday the 13th" movies, the person answering can be allowed to say just "all the Friday the 13th movies." This quickens the round.

If a "judge" or game master is selected, they can have the final word on a questionable answer. Otherwise, all the players must agree that any given answer is appropriate. The players and the Judge should be wary of sneaky players who may be using "Google" to retrieve quick answers. You can usually tell when some one is doing this.

Have fun with it and make new friends!


Poetic Justice!

"Poetic Justice" is a fun, social party game and conversation starter. It was designed for two or more people and is specially fun with large groups. Play it at parties and other social events, or use it to get to know that new romantic interest! You can have hours of fun playing the game as is, or coming up with your own "house rules." It is simple to play, fast and fun!

The original game comes loaded with one starter subject - "General #1." Please visit the Minty Goodness shop In-World or on Marketplace for more card expansions! "General" is a sampling of mixed subjects.

Poetic Justice is extremely easy to use. After purchase, rez the box and right click to open it. Copy the contents into your inventory. When you are ready to play it, drag the game out and position it to your taste. The game device can be sat on the floor or on a table or any surface you wish. Just make sure everyone can click on it.

Once out, (and your fellow players are situated and ready), click on the ink well of the game. This will bring up the Subject Card Menu. Only the Owner can click on the base to change the subject cards. Choose what subject you want to play. (Only General #1 will appear in new games.) You can change the subject as often as you wish. You may want to add expansion Subject Cards in the future. When you add them to you game device, they will show up in your menu too. You can create your own cards, or purchase pre-made cards and card sets from Minty Good Games.

After the subject is agreed upon, players may take turns right clicking on feather quill. Every time someone clicks on the feather, they will be given a starting line of a poem. They now need to add their line, hopefully rhyming with the given line. There is no score keeping, or winners in this game. You simply have fun adding your line of poetry. There are many ways of playing and styles of poetry that can be used. (Maybe players agree on Prose, instead of Rhyme?) Everyone should give their line after the first player's line, again, rhyming with the device given line. A lot of laughs can be had as players add their silly or serious lines to the growing poem!

Try to keep to the beat of the poem too, if possible. Perhaps you can get double "points" for rhyming multiple words within the same line! If the Owner wants to award points, he or she can, which may lead to the "Best Poet" award!

Play continues until all players are finished taking turns. There really is no "end" to the game. Poem lines are drawn in order, so there should not be any repeats during a game. Be creative! Have fun!
