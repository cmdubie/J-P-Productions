The town of Sheffield offers a great deal of versatility when it comes to open or organized role playing. Everyone is welcome to role play here. You will find all the amenities to make your role playing experience fun and exciting!

Guidelines:

- Please abide by both the Larimore Estate and Sheffield's Covenants. You will find the covenants in-world under "World/About Land/Covenant." Read them, understand them and there will be no problems!

- The Sheffield sim is open to all, for both "open play" role playing, as well as scheduled, Game Master ran events. If there is a conflict of availability of space or time, the scheduled games will have priority. Please respect these groups and leave them alone. There are many scheduled games available for you to play in here. Just ask a Game Master or Land Manager when and where they get together!

- The Sheffield sim has been designed for any of the following role play scenarios:

- Victorian Era (Late 1800's)
- Steampunk Era (Late 1800's to early 1900's
- Gas Light Era (Late 1800's)
- Sherlock Holmes Era (Early 1900's)
- H.P. Lovecraft Eras (Late 1800's to 1930's)
- War Eras (Pre WWI to Late WWII)
- Gothic - Yes, you can play vampire or werewolf here, just do not disturb others engaged in other activity.
- Poe, Vernes and other great authors from the turn of the century eras.

- Please respect the era and dress, role play, and act accordingly. There are many fine shops both in Sheffield and around the grid that sell appropriate clothing and gear. Try to "play the part" and bring these exciting and adventurous times to life! Sheffield was deliberately created to be "generic," to allow any game rules to be play here.

- Absolutely no child play, griefing, spamming, too many attached scripts, open nudity, open sexual acts, excessive swearing, sexual solicitations, and anything else that would disrupt in other's fun.

- The Damage function may be turned on occasionally. You will know when it is on when you see a small heart on the top of your viewer's screen. Please IM Zoltan Mint if you desire this feature turned on/off.

- Please get permission from Zoltan Mint if you wish to implement the use of combat HUDs. Though we do not mind these HUDs, we would like to know when they are being used.

- Please refrain from flying, teleporting, and camming while here. These items are not turned off, but by not doing them, you are preserving the atmosphere!

- Please do not bother the shoppers or shop owners in any of the commercial buildings in Sheffield. Please stay out of the private apartment in the apartment building on the coast. This is a private residency that can be rented if desired.

- Please donate what lindens you can. If you like this place and use it to play your games, your donations will help keep it open!

- Have fun, socialize and play nice! This is what Second Life was meant for!


Things to do in Sheffield

Please feel free to use any of the available locations listed below to play out your games in Sheffield! Here is a list of some of the locations you will find on this sim!

- The Public Park - Features an 8 person gathering camp fire, to meet and chat, plus a pergola that allows you to dance, eat or relax with up to 7 other friends!

- The Arcade - You will find all types of games to play here. Poker, roulette, Zyngo, Greedy, Skeeball, air hockey, and much more! Don't forget to check out the 3rd floor!

- The Docks - Here you will find plenty of areas to role play those shady encounters. Check out the huge freighter currently docked there. (The ship even has hulls to explore!) The Zombie Apocalypse Head Quarters in located here as well.

- The Lighthouse - Many rooms to play in and explore - climb to the highest floor and see the best vantage point. This place also holds the Kingspoint Radio Station.

- The Police Station - Houses the law of the land. Ask Zoltan Mint and he can give you permission to lock people up in one of the cells there. Prisoners can not get out in any normal way when incarcerated.

- The Hospital - This big building houses an Emergency Ward, patience beds, and has an awesome Psychiatric Ward. (Perfect for those sanity checks!)

- Paint Ball - This is turned on by request. Please IM Zoltan Mint for tournaments or casual play! Free paint guns for all!

- Zombie Apocalypse - Turned on by request. The name says it all - 100's of roaming, brain hungry undead!

- The Church - Can be used for weddings, funerals, baptisms, or other role play needs.

- The Graveyard - Creepy place, also used in horror role play scenarios.

- The Remmingtom Haunted Mansion - This is a "game in a game!" Can you solve the mystery? This haunted mansion is full of intrigue and mystery. Click on the Information Board near the entrance for full instructions on how to play..

- Hole In The Wall Bar - Cute little gathering place with the old world feel.

- Back Alleys - Great place to gather, chat, and plan nefarious things! Make sure you click on the various items to interact with them.

- The Amherst Hotel - A functioning hotel with 2 rooms on a second floor. Use this as a gathering place for games that take your characters out of town!

- Miskatonic University (Sheffield Campus) - This huge building is full of things to do and use! You will find a quaint cafe (each table serves food), painting class room, a laboratory, small cabaret theater, a great library, a class room, lecture hall with movie screen, and many more things! Secret - can you find the hidden attic entrance?

There are many more interactive things for you to play with in Sheffield. Explore and have fun!
