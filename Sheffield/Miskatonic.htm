Miskatonic University, (the Sheffield Campus), is a traditional college of learning that is for all scholars seeking a higher education. Specializing in occult sciences and fundamental research in the super natural sciences, MU is leading the way in studies involving exploration into all things possible.

This ancient edifice is a branch of the main University in Arkham, Massachusetts. Slightly smaller, and far more personable, MU in Sheffield leads the way in launching expeditions and clinical work in the meta sciences.

Explore the building and each room inside this wonderful place of learning. There is much to do and see here. Students are encouraged to look for future courses and expeditions announced on campus, and join us in discovering the dark secrets that lie just below the surface of life!


What to do at Miskatonic University

Here is a list of things you can do in MU, in no particular order.

- Speak to the talking parrot. It actually has artificial inelegance and can carry on a long and meaningful conversation! - Ground Floor

- See the antique music box! - Ground Floor

- View and try to identify all the famous Author's portraits! They are all from the same era. - Ground Floor

- Enjoy the model train display! The train layout and the trains themselves will change often, so make sure you return to see this awesome ancient toy! - Ground Floor

- Visit the Art Room, where you can paint a picture and actually keep the finished painting! View some of the latest artwork from famous Cthulhu dreamers and artists! - Ground Floor

- Attend a session in the spacious classroom. The chairs have many sit animation's, as does the lectern. Oh, and leave a message on the chalkboard! - Ground Floor

- Use the wonderful library for study and research. Plus, there are many nooks and crannies that allows you to cuddle up with a good book, regardless of that book's macabre contents! - Ground Floor

- Make sure you grab a bite to eat at the campus cafe! Sit at any of the tables and click just right of the placement and you will get a complete menu of appearing food entries! - Ground Floor

- Be sure to take advantage of the small movie theater! The screen is for public use and you can access movies, TV shows, and videos off of Youtube and other sites. Feel free to use this room for lectures on your own expeditions! - Second Floor

- Use the laboratory to research all your projects and conduct experiments as you see fit. Please clean up all body parts after you are done! - Second Floor

- You are welcome to take advantage of the wonderful photography studio! You will find couples poses and individual male and female pose stands as well. Click through the 100's of background scenes to find the perfect back drop for your portrait! You can even change the lighting colors and ambiance. - Second Floor

- Make sure to take in an act at MU's very own cabaret theater! We hope to schedule in lots of different acts on this stage so check back often! If you have an act, contact Zoltan Mint and lets get you on that stage! Oh, and by the way, the piano plays music! Try it out! - Second Floor

- If you can find a way into the secret attic, MU stores all its creepy research projects up there. Though not easy to get to, once found, you will be amazed at the findings of the college's research department. (There is even a rumor that they have a time travel machine! - Third Floor
