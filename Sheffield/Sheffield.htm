The town of Sheffield is a full region virtual world in Second Life, that offers a lot of fun and exciting entertainment. Sheffield is a Middle 20th Century, East Coast American town, catering to explorers and enthusiasts of HP Lovecraft, Edgar Allen Poe, Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. The town is perfect for Victorian, Gaslight, and Steampunk intrigue. Designed for role players, Sheffield has a 1900's to 1940's era design, that includes many areas to stage great games or to explore and have fun.

Sheffield is home to the popular "Jack's Nightclub," as well as many other interesting places including; a tavern, a sheriff's office, a hospital, a university, a lighthouse radio station, a haunted mansion, a church, a dock with ships, and a gaming arcade as well.

Sheffield has rentable and affordable apartments and quaint business shops to lease as well. The folks of Sheffield frequently schedule game events, festival celebrations, parties, live entertainers, contests, and many other exciting events every week. Make sure you stop by and explore Sheffield thoroughly. There is a lot to do and interact with, and who knows... You may uncover some rather extraordinary secrets!
