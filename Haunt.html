You Will Believe!

Haunt is a company of professional performers who love to entertain audiences with inventive magical illusions, special effects and good old fashion ghost stories.

We will travel to any location, bringing props, audio and visual equipment, and other frightening devices to create an evening of fun and fright. Although private homes are best, we can adapt to almost any location, creating an atmosphere of trepidation and intrigue. We can find ghosts in the most unlikely places like homes, hotels, bed and breakfasts, motels, and even rental halls.

You and your guests will participate in a ghost hunt, room to room, experiment to experiment, uncovering evidence and experiencing contact with the undead.

You won't believe your eyes as ghosts play tricks on your friends and family, speak from beyond, and perhaps even appear in your event's location. Experiments are performed with shocking results, and everyone gets involved in this highly entertaining ghost hunt!

Trust us, at the end of the evening, you will believe!

How to Plan a Haunt!

The Haunt event is perfect for intimate parties throughout the year. Obviously, Haunt is extremely popular around the Halloween season. If you are thinking about a very special Halloween party using Haunt, please contact us at least a month or more in advance. A Haunt event is something that none of your guests will ever forget. Everybody is involved and right in the action. Our professional actors will convincingly entertain everyone at your event! This show draws people together both in trepidation and with laughs.

When you schedule us for a show, we arrive at your location early and go over the details with you. We set up our props and ghost hunting equipment and prepare for an evening your guest will never forget! When all your friends and/or family arrive, we begin our ghost hunt. A Host will travel with your guests to several different rooms, doing experiments to track the "paranormal activities" in each location. Your guests will be mystified and probably alarmed as "proof positive" is the result! Everyone will get involved, becoming ghost hunters and feeling the ghostly evidence of the netherworlds!

They will hear ghostly sounds, see paranormal activity, examine ethereal evidence and participate in spirit tests that just may have them believing in ghosts! The evening will end with a great séance, where a trained psychic will attempt to contact the land of the dead and put the spirits to rest. This climatic, theatrical ending will have your guests screaming in delight!

Haunt is appropriate for no less than 5 guests or more than 40. This is an intimate show, so too many guests and the show loses its magic. The perfect sized crowd is around 25.

Please note: Haunt is not appropriate for children under 16 years of age, or anyone in poor health or may be easily frightened.

Haunt Logistics!

For Haunt to optimally work at your next party, we do have some basic requirements. Please contact us if you have any questions or special requests.

  1. - Haunt must be performed indoors. Though we can work halls and other public places, we prefer private homes or hotels. Bed & Breakfast Inns are ideal!

  2. - We need 3-4 separate rooms. Haunt is a ghost hunt and we need to roam as a group, freely throughout the evening. Please make several different rooms accessible. They can include living rooms, dining rooms, bedrooms, attics, basements, garages, etc... Keep in mind that your entire guest count will be in these rooms, all at the same time. We will be setting up special magical props in these rooms, so please keep your guests out of these rooms until the performance times. Your personal Haunt Rep will go over all the details of the logistics of the evening.

  3. - One of the areas needs to have a table. A dining room table, cleared of debris is best. You may set up a table of any size, anywhere in your house, but because of the séance, a table is necessary. Also, we will need at least one electrical outlet in this room.

  4. - You may serve food either before or after this two hour show. Please, no formal dining during the show. Munchies and drinks are fine, but you don't want your guests missing anything! And yes, we encourage your guest to imbibe "spirits," just as long as they are not too "spirited" to miss the fun!

  5. - We need at least a half hour set up time before the performance, uninterrupted by guests. If you or your guests watch the set up, it will spoil the magic and your fun. Be sure to emphasize to your guests to arrive no earlier than the appointed party time.

  6. - Haunt is a professional entertainment company that is insured by a contractual agreement. For your safety and ours, we will mail you a contract that guaranties your event's date. Please, as with all party entertainment, make sure your house is "party safe.
